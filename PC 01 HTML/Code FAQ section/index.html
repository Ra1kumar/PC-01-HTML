Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How long can I access my content?

After enrolling, you have unlimited access to this course content for as long as you like. You can access all the Lessons, Videos and Tasks.

The course is a right mix of videos, text lessons, live mentoring sessions, peer review discussions and writing exercises. In addition, there will be writing workshops with our writing coaches for better understanding.

Once you enrol for the course, you'll have all the required access. For better learning outcomes we reveal the study material and text lessons week by week.
